ABOUT US

VWM Solutions is an Austrian company founded in 2013 offering ColiMinder devices for fully automated microbiological contamination measurements within 15 minutes.

ColiMinder enables process monitoring and control for all water related applications. Microbiological contamination measurements drastically increases the efficiency and the safety of water treatment processes and distribution networks.

ColiMinder's reliability has been proven since early 2014 in different applications from drinking water to waste water all around the globe. The technology is based on direct measurements of specific metabolic activities of the target organisms.
